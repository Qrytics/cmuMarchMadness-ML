Bracket Types

📋 Regular Bracket

All predictions are made before the tournament starts based on seedings and season statistics. Winners of later rounds are predicted based on predicted winners of earlier rounds. This is the traditional bracket format.

⚡ Progressive Bracket

After each round, new predictions are made based on the actual winners. This allows the model to adapt to upsets. This format typically scores higher as errors don't cascade through the bracket.

Historical Backtest

Simulated bracket scores on past tournament seasons (trained only on prior seasons' data).
